# Commercial Demonstration Script

This script is valid only after an isolated staging environment is migrated, seeded, and its credentials are verified.

1. Sign in as the renter and search the illustrative Abuja rental catalogue.
2. Open The Courtyard Residence, save it, inspect the virtual tour, and review the clear illustrative-content notice.
3. Open Messages and show the four-message renter-agent history and confirmed inspection.
4. Sign in as the agent and show the received lead, schedule, portfolio freshness, and rental flyer.
5. Open the flyer PDF and scan its QR code to confirm the attributed property target.
6. Sign in as the developer and show available, reserved, and sold units plus the payment-plan request.
7. Sign in as the hotel administrator and show the reservation request, airport-pickup thread, and availability guard.
8. Sign in as the agency administrator and show invitation acceptance, team membership, lead assignment, and pilot plan.
9. Sign in as the platform administrator and show approve, revise, reject, suspend, reinstate, and audit records.

Do not present any fictional identity, business, rating, conversation, or property as a real customer record.
